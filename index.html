Vaccine Mandate Decision Aid Tool

Explore and compare vaccine mandate scenarios based on public preferences (n = 322 pilot).

How to Use: In Inputs, select severity (mild/severe), attribute levels, and “Lives Saved.” Then click Calculate. Results appear in each tab. Save scenarios under Scenarios.

Introduction

This tool applies your error-component-logit coefficients (pooled/mild/severe) from n = 322 pilot respondents to model how attribute changes—scope, exemption, coverage, lives saved—affect uptake, WTSL, and net benefits.
